How does it work?

Applications for our 2021 cohort open on April 19th and closes on April 30th. Submissions will be reviewed on a rolling basis.

The top 90 applicants will be selected for a 3-week probation period, after which 30 performing applicants will be accepted to be a part of this year’s 3-month program.

The Mentorship program lasts for 3 months and the 2021 cohort will take place from June through Early September.
